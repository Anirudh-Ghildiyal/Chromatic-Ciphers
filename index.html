CHROMATIC CIPHERS

Welcome

Kindly go through these instructions for the game:

  • There will be three rounds to the game with increasing difficulties.
  • You will be shown few cards with different colors and numbers on them. Memorize the numbers associated with those colors.
  • After a certain period of time, the cards will again be displayed on the screen, but this time without the numbers on them. There will also be mathematical operators shown between the cards.
  • You must solve the equation represented by the cards and submit your answer in the text box given below.
  • The answer shall always be an integer value, never a fraction.
  • On submission of each correct answer, you'll receive a score point.
  • Submission of wrong answer will lead to game over.
